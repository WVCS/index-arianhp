Maxime Bouzid: A Sponsored Rider

Overview

Maxime Bouzid is a French scooter rider that grew up in the suburbs of Paris. He is currently 24 years old and has been riding since 2010. When Maxime was still a kid, he had broken his arm because of a type of cancer he had. Unfortunately, the doctor informed Maxime that he would never be able to ride again. Present day, 2021, Maxime Bouzid is a professional rider sponsored by AO Scooters with his own signature deck.
